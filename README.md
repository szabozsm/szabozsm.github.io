## PlainWallet

PlainWallet is a free, open-source Android app for storing your membership, loyalty, discount, and store cards in one simple place.

I built it because every alternative I tried was either bloated with features I didn't need, missing the ones I actually did, or packed with ads on top of it all. So I made something clean, straightforward, and complete instead. There are no ads, no paywalls, and no subscriptions. Just a simple, free tool that does exactly what it promises.

PlainWallet is not yet available on the Google Play Store due to some technical difficulties I am currently working through, but you can find the full project and download it directly from GitHub:

[github.com/szabozsm/PlainWallet](https://github.com/szabozsm/PlainWallet)
